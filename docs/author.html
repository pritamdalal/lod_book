About the Author

Pritam Dalal founded Options Data Science in 2018. Prior to that he held a variety of trading and analysis roles at firms including Cargill, Wolverine Trading, Two Harbors Investment Corp, and Allianz Investment Management.

Pritam is an adjunct professor in the Mathematics Department at the University of Minnesota, where he teaches R and Python to graduate students.

Pritam holds a Bachelors in Mathematics and Economics from the University of California, Berkeley as well as a Masters in Financial Mathematics from the University of Minnnesota, Twin Cities.
